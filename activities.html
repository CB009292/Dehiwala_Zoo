Activities

Feed the Animals

During feeding times, you may catch our animals at their most active. Listen to our caretakers tell their stories and even feed the animals. Learn more about our flora and fauna from the experts - the keepers! Hear fascinating animal tales and get the opportunity to feed some of them.

Sea Lion Show

Sea Lion Show provides oceans of entertainment for the entire family! The stars of the zoo's sea lion show have returned to the stage. Olympic-style dives on a high-diving platform are among the acrobatic and athletic actions you'll see from these clever animals. Powerful swimming and there will be plenty of splashing and even more surprises! Our trainers discuss sea lion behavior and positive-reinforcement training, animal fun facts, and the need of preserving the sea lions' ocean environment while on stage with the animals.

Elephant Show

Elephant Show displays the majestic nature of these mega herbivores. Elephant keeper-presenters, who look after the elephants, will give fascinating animal facts and information on a regular basis. The animal presentation is expected to be an educational experience that promotes and spreads conservation awareness. Come see for yourself how smart, social, strong, and yet kind elephants can be.
